# Cover email — Therapists / SLPs / educators (interview prospects)

**Purpose:** Sent ahead of an interview. Frame the shared deck around *their* daily reality and make it easy to say yes to a conversation. Warm, concrete, not salesy.

**Subject line options**
- An AI AAC app I'd love your eyes on (before we talk)
- Building a communication board in seconds — a quick look before our chat
- Would this save you hours? A 5-minute look at BlasterAI

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for making time to talk with me about [BlasterAI / AAC / non-verbal communication]. Ahead of our conversation, I wanted to share a short deck and a couple of demo videos so we can spend our time on your reactions rather than on setup.

Quick context: **BlasterAI is a free, open-source AAC app for non-verbal children.** A child taps picture tiles and it speaks a natural, age-appropriate sentence — and when they tap the same tile again and again, it escalates the urgency, the way a child who really means it would. The part I most want your read on: a therapist can **describe a goal in plain English** — *"feelings for a 5-year-old working on frustration vs anger"* — and get a **complete, editable, shareable board in seconds**, instead of hunting through thousands of vocabulary items.

I'm not trying to sell you anything — it's open source and free. I'm trying to find out where AI genuinely helps in your work and, just as importantly, **where it gets in the way**. Your honest reaction is the whole point.

A few things I'd especially value your take on:
- Does the generated-sentence approach fit how the kids you work with actually communicate?
- Is single-word (classic) mode important enough that AI should stay fully optional? *(It's a per-child toggle today.)*
- Would scene generation actually save you time, or does the hand-crafting matter clinically?

**Attached:** the deck (PDF), a one-page summary, and short demo videos (~1 min each). Watch as much or as little as you like before we talk.

Looking forward to it — and thank you for the work you do.
