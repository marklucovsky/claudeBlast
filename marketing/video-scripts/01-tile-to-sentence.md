# Video 1 — Tile → sentence (the "aha")

**Goal:** In under a minute, show a viewer who's never seen AAC exactly what BlasterAI does: a child taps a few pictures, and a real, spoken sentence comes out.
**Length:** 45–60s · **Orientation:** portrait · **Driver:** `demo_basic.yaml` (playback) or live taps.

## Notes for the editor
- The **magic beat is 0:13** — the first spoken sentence. Give it air; don't rush the cut. Consider a subtle zoom on the tray as the text appears.
- Show the **color-coded tiles** clearly in the opening — it signals thoughtful design.
- If using `demo_basic.yaml`, the exact sentences depend on the live model; caption whatever it actually says. The examples above are representative.
- Keep the child's finger/cursor visible on taps so the cause→effect is obvious.

## The one line if someone only reads the caption
> *Tap a few pictures → hear a real sentence. That's BlasterAI.*
